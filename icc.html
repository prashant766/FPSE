ICC Certification Courses

Prepare for International Code Council (ICC) certifications with expert-led training programs.

ICC Fire Inspector I +
  • Computer-based exam (Pearson VUE + online proctored)
  • Duration: ~2 hours
  • ~60 MCQs
  • Based on IFC
  • For entry-level inspectors
  • No strict experience required
  • Focus: inspection & compliance
  • Widely accepted in projects
ICC Fire Inspector II +
  • Computer-based exam
  • Duration: ~2–3 hours
  • ~80 MCQs
  • Advanced IFC
  • For experienced inspectors
  • Requires Level I knowledge
  • Focus: complex inspections
  • Higher authority role
ICC Fire Plans Examiner (F3) +
  • Computer-based exam
  • Duration: ~3 hours
  • ~80–90 MCQs
  • Based on IFC + design codes
  • For engineers & consultants
  • Requires strong experience
  • Focus: plan review & approval
  • High demand role
NEBOSH IGC +
  • Online exam + practical
  • 24 hr window + report
  • Scenario-based
  • For beginners
  • No experience needed
  • Focus: risk & safety management
  • Globally accepted
IOSH Managing Safely +
  • Online/center exam
  • ~45 min + practical
  • ~30 MCQs
  • For managers
  • No experience needed
  • Short course (3–5 days)
OSHA Safety Programs +
  • Online training
  • 10hr / 30hr
  • Basic quizzes
  • For workers & staff
  • Focus: workplace safety
BCSP ASP +
  • Pearson VUE exam
  • ~5 hours
  • ~200 MCQs
  • For degree holders
  • Pre-requisite for CSP
  • Advanced safety level
BCSP CSP +
  • Pearson VUE exam
  • ~5.5 hours
  • ~200 MCQs
  • For experienced professionals
  • Top-level certification
  • Highly valued globally
