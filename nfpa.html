NFPA Certification Courses

Advance your career with globally recognized fire protection certifications.

Certified Fire Protection Specialist (CFPS) +
  • Globally recognized certification in fire protection & prevention
  • Supports career growth, promotions & salary increases
  • Gold standard credential in fire industry
  • Based on Fire Protection Handbook (21st Edition)
  • Exam: 3-hour, open-book, 100 MCQs
  • Conducted worldwide via Prometric
Certified Fire Inspector (CFI) +
  • Validates fire inspection & code enforcement competence
  • Based on NFPA 1030 standards
  • Covers NFPA 1, 13, 25, 72, 101
  • Exam: 3-hour, open-book, 72 MCQs
  • Conducted globally at Prometric centers
Certified Fire Inspector I (CFI-I) +
  • Basic fire inspection certification (NFPA 1031)
  • For inspectors, contractors & safety professionals
  • Exam: 4-hour, open-book, 100 MCQs
  • Requires practicum approval
Certified Fire Inspector II (CFI-II) +
  • Advanced inspection certification
  • Requires CFI-I
  • Exam: 4-hour, open-book
  • Recognized globally
Certified Fire Plan Examiner (CFPE) +
  • Focus on fire plan review & compliance
  • Based on NFPA codes
  • Exam: 3-hour, open-book
  • Used by engineers & consultants
Certified Fire Alarm ITM Specialist (CFAITMS) +
  • Focus on fire alarm inspection & maintenance
  • Based on NFPA 72
  • Exam: 3-hour, open-book
Certified Hazard Recognition Specialist (CHRS) +
  • Focus on hazard recognition
  • Based on NFPA 101
  • Exam: 3-hour, open-book
Water-Based Fire Protection ITM (WBITM) +
  • Inspection & maintenance of water systems
  • Based on NFPA 25
  • Exam: 2.5-hour, open-book
