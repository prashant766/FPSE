NICET Certification

Advance your career with NICET certification programs in fire protection systems and inspection.

Fire Alarm Systems Certification (Levels I – IV) +
  • Computer-based exam (Pearson VUE centers)
  • Duration: ~2–3 hours per level
  • MCQs (~85–120 questions)
  • Based on NFPA 72 & related standards
  • For technicians, engineers, installers, designers
  • Requires work experience + Level progression
  • Focus: design, installation, testing, maintenance
  • Globally recognized credential
Inspection & Testing of Fire Alarm Systems (ITFAS) +
  • Computer-based exam (Pearson VUE centers)
  • Duration: ~2–3 hours
  • MCQs (~90–110 questions)
  • Based on NFPA 72 (ITM)
  • For inspectors, maintenance staff, technicians
  • Requires relevant field experience
  • Focus: inspection, testing, troubleshooting
  • High demand in ITM roles
Inspection & Testing of Water-Based Systems (Levels I – III) +
  • Computer-based exam (Pearson VUE centers)
  • Duration: ~2–3 hours per level
  • MCQs (~90–110 questions)
  • Based on NFPA 25
  • For sprinkler inspectors, ITM technicians
  • Requires system experience
  • Focus: inspection, testing, maintenance
  • Comparable to NFPA WBITM roles
Special Hazards Systems (Levels I – IV) +
  • Computer-based exam (Pearson VUE centers)
  • Duration: ~2–3 hours per level
  • MCQs (~85–110 questions)
  • Based on NFPA (clean agent, foam, gas)
  • For engineers, designers, specialists
  • Requires experience + progression
  • Focus: design & installation
  • Used in oil & gas, data centers, industries
