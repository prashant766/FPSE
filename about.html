⭐ About Us → (What we are)

Fire Protection Solutions & Engineering is a specialized training and consultancy institute dedicated to elevating fire safety, security, computer courses and risk management standards across industries.

We deliver globally recognized technical training, Preparation courses, skill development programs and professional fire system inspection services based on NFPA Codes, Indian Building Codes, and international best practices.

With expert trainers and real world field experience, we help professionals, organizations, and students build strong competencies in fire protection engineering, safety management, and security system technologies and computer class training

🌍 Why Choose Us

  • Industry-certified global instructors
  • Real incident-based practical teaching
  • Code-accurate, standard driven technical content
  • Hands on training with actual systems
  • Support for exam preparation, career guidance & job readiness

💼 Who We Train

We support a diverse range of professionals, organizations and industries that rely on robust fire protection systems, safety excellence and technical competency. Our training programs are designed to address both individual professional development and organizational compliance requirements across multiple sectors.

👤 Professionals & Individuals

  • Students and Engineering Students
  • Safety Officers and Safety Professionals
  • Emergency Responders
  • Facility and Operations Managers
  • Industrial and Non Industrial Workers
  • Professionals seeking specialization in fire, safety, and security systems
